About the Course

What the course is about

This course will serve as your starting point for UI/UX Design, helping you cut through the complexity.

It is designed for anyone who want to add UI/UX Design to their skill set or change jobs to work in that industry.

What you will learn

  • Intro to User Experience
  • Design Thinking
  • User Research
  • Designing for Inclusion
  • Laws of UX and Best Practices
  • Color Theory
  • Design Systems
  • Creative & Art Direction
  • Grids & Layout Design
